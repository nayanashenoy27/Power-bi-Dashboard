# Project Title
## HR Analytics Dashboard: Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights (Power BI)
# Project Objective
To analyze employee data and identify key factors influencing attrition by examining demographics, salary, job roles, and tenure. The objective is to uncover patterns and provide actionable insights that help organizations improve employee retention and make data-driven HR decisions.
## 📂 Dataset Used
[HR Analytics Dataset](https://github.com/nayanashenoy27/Power-bi-Dashboard/blob/main/HR_Analytics.csv)
## Questions KPI
- What is the total number of employees in the organization?
- How many employees have left the company (attrition count)?
- What is the overall attrition rate (%)?
- What is the average age of employees?
- What is the average salary of employees?
- What is the average tenure (years at company)?
- Which job role has the highest attrition?
- Which salary slab has the highest attrition?
- Which age group experiences the most attrition?
